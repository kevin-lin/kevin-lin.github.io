Commitmnt - A Tool to Help You Reach Your Goals

A tool to help you keep track of and reach your goals

Web App

Commitmnt is my most recent pet project that is still in the works. Each person has a public record that shows statistics on the number of commitments they complete. The user is encouraged to make commitments that challenge themselves. If the user fails to complete the commitment they made, it will show on their public record. Users are motivated to complete their commitments in order to maintain a positive public record.

Merry Christmas 2013 - Hacking the Christmas Card

This was my quick holiday hack for 2013. Recipients were mailed a christmas card with a unique 4 color combination on the front. Inside of each card was a link to a webapp hosted on a heroku repository - "merry-christmas.herokuapp.com". On that website, recipients were expected to enter their unique 4 color code and a custom christmas card was pulled up for them on the webpage.

UT SURE Walk - Keeping the UT Community Safe

UT SURE Walk is a student run volunteer organization that provides walks to and from the University of Texas campus at night. Currently, if you wish to request a walk, you need to call a phone number and speak to an operator. I worked on a web app to automate the request process. UT SURE Walk wishes to implement this new automated process in the future.
